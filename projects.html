Working with Aircraft Maintenance Engineering (AME) students at Aeronautical Institute of Bangladesh

Worked to ensure a continuous and smooth education system during the COVID-19 pandemic. Also, collaborated with other universities for ensuring better job placements for our students.

MISHTI KOTHA ('Mishti'=Sweet, 'Kotha'=Words)
‘Digital Khichuri’ - A Social Case Competition: powered by UNDP and StartUp Bangladesh

June 2020 - July 2020

The task was to propose a feasible solution to fight the discrimination and social stigma against the front-line workers of Bangladesh during the COVID-19 pandemic. So, our team developed a website and surveyed 100+ people. We also made a video tutorial to educate the people around us.

Unmanned, Electrically powered and Radio-controlled Aircraft
AIAA DBF Competition

November 2018 - April 2019

The desire to win the 2019 AIAA DBF competition lead to this project which presents an attempt to design, fabricate and demonstrate the flight capabilities of a folded surface multi-purpose aircraft to support carrier operations. The aircraft is built according to specific requirements of the competition. Upon designing in SolidWorks, the aircraft is built.
